## Explanation of architecture
The Aegis-G architecture follows a 3-layer approach:
1. Ingestion & Adversarial Shield
2. Cognitive Analysis (Agents)
3. Inter-agency Trust & Compliance

## Modules/components description
- **Agent 1**: Forensic Investigator (DeBERTa + Stylometry)
- **Agent 2**: Temporal Graph Oracle
- **Agent 3**: Intelligence Fusion & RAG Memory
- **Agent 4**: Policy Guardian
