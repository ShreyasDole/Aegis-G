## Short Description 
Aegis-G is an enterprise-grade Cognitive Defense System designed to identify, attribute, and neutralize LLM-driven Malign Information Operations (MIO) at scale. It monitors semantic narratives, topological propagation, and linguistic artifacts to ensure zero-trust compliance.

## Problem Statement
Modern cyber threats are no longer just malicious code; they are malicious narratives generated by LLMs. An enterprise system is required to attribute and explain AI-generated disinformation effectively.

## Objectives
- Multi-Class Attribution of AI vs Human text
- Token-level explainability (SHAP)
- RAG-Powered Institutional Memory for narrative matching
